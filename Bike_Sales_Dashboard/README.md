Bike Sales Performance Dashboard

#Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze bike sales performance across different countries, customer demographics, product categories, and time periods.

The dashboard provides business insights through KPIs, charts, maps, and interactive slicers, allowing users to explore sales performance dynamically.

#Dashboard Preview

(screenshots/dashboard-overview.jpg)

#Objectives

- Analyze total sales performance
- Monitor profit and profit margin
- Compare revenue across countries
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Explore customer demographics
- Provide interactive filtering for deeper analysis

#Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin

#Dashboard Visualizations

- Revenue by Month
- Revenue by Country (Map)
- Revenue by Product Category
- Revenue by Gender
- Revenue by Age Group

#Interactive Filters

- Country
- Year
- Product Category
